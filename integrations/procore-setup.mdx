## Prerequisites

- You are a **Procore Company Admin** for the company you want to connect.
- You have received a **FieldGrid App Version Key** (a 36‑character ID).
- You have a **FieldGrid account with management access**.

## Install FieldGrid in Procore

1. Sign in to Procore at `https://procore.com`.
2. Switch to the correct **Company**.
3. Go to **Company level → Admin → App Management**.
4. Click **Install App → Install Custom App**.
5. Paste the **FieldGrid App Version Key** and click **Install** (confirm any prompts).
6. _(Optional)_ Create an **App Configuration** and apply it to **Active and Future Projects**.

## Connect Procore in FieldGrid

1. In FieldGrid, go to **Admin → Integrations → Procore**.
2. Click **Connect Procore**.
3. You’ll be redirected to Procore (`https://login.procore.com`).
4. Log in with a Procore user that can see projects in that company and approve the FieldGrid app.
5. After redirecting back to FieldGrid, the Procore card should show **Connected**.
6. Click **Sync Projects** (or **Sync All**) to pull initial data.

## Troubleshooting

- **403 / 404 on projects**: usually means the app is not installed in that company or your user doesn’t have permission to see **Projects**.
- **Not authenticated on callback**: ensure you’re using the in‑app **Connect Procore** button, not typing the URL directly.

If you get stuck at any point, you can contact **FieldGrid Support** at `support@fieldgrid.com`.
