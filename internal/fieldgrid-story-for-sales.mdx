## Who this is for

This page is for **sales, customer success, and onboarding** teammates who:

- Run demos for prospects.
- Guide new customers through their first weeks on Fieldgrid.
- Need a simple way to reuse the product docs we’ve written.

## Core story arc

At a high level, Fieldgrid helps teams:

1. **Know what’s happening now** – jobs, schedules, and status.
2. **Guide work consistently** – clear instructions, checklists, and resources.
3. **Capture what actually happened** – time, materials, notes, photos.
4. **Turn work into revenue and insight** – invoicing, analytics, and AI-powered understanding.

You can adapt the emphasis depending on the audience (operations, technicians, leadership).

## Suggested demo flow – admin story

Use these public docs as your narrative spine:

1. **Intro and concepts**
   - Start from:
     - `index.mdx` – **What is Fieldgrid?**
     - `concepts.mdx` – **Fieldgrid concepts**.
   - Highlight:
     - Customer → Project → Job → Work Order → Jobsite.

2. **Admin dashboard**
   - Docs: **Admin dashboard and navigation**, **Solutions – service managers/admins**.
   - Show:
     - Dashboard overview.
     - Sidebar navigation.
     - How to jump into Jobs, Schedule, and Invoices.

3. **Job lifecycle**
   - Docs: **Admin job lifecycle and work orders**.
   - Story:
     - Create a job under a project.
     - Add work orders.
     - Assign a technician.
     - Track progress and close out the job.

4. **Time, inventory, and billing**
   - Docs:
     - **Technician time tracking**.
     - **Admin inventory management**.
     - **Admin invoicing and billing**.
   - Show:
     - How technician actions (time, parts, expenses) roll up into billing.
     - How admins see the full picture and generate invoices.

5. **Intelligence and communication**
   - Docs:
     - **Notifications and announcements**.
     - **AI assistant (Alex)**.
   - Show:
     - How the system keeps people informed.
     - How Alex can quickly summarize or answer questions about work.

## When to share docs with customers

Good times to send specific docs:

- **Evaluation / pre‑sale**
  - `index.mdx`, **Solutions by role**, and a couple of key workflow guides.
- **New admin onboarding**
  - **Admin getting started**, **Admin onboarding**, **Fieldgrid concepts**.
- **New technician onboarding**
  - **Technician getting started**, **Technician daily workflow**.
- **Self‑serve help**
  - **FAQ** plus relevant troubleshooting pages when questions come up.

Always send the smallest set of links that fully answers the customer’s question—avoid overwhelming people with too many pages at once.

## Keeping this aligned

When you adjust demos or see new patterns in customer questions:

- Update:
  - This page (for story structure).
  - The corresponding Product docs, if gaps exist.
- Coordinate with whoever owns docs to keep everything in sync.
