title: "Jobs and work orders runbook"
description: "Internal runbook for handling issues in the job and work order lifecycle."
---

## Scope

Use this runbook when:

- Jobs or work orders appear missing, duplicated, or stuck in an incorrect status.
- Technicians cannot see assignments they expect to see.
- Admin views do not reflect recent changes.

## 1. Triage

- Clarify:
  - Which customer, project, job, and (if relevant) work order are affected.
  - What the expected status or behavior is.
  - Whether the issue is limited to certain users or roles.
- Ask for:
  - Screenshots where possible.
  - Time window when the issue was first noticed.

## 2. Visibility issues (user can’t see work)

- Confirm:
  - The user’s role and organization.
  - That the job or work order is assigned to the correct technician or team.
- Check:
  - Filters and date ranges in the UI (e.g., is the list filtered to a different status or date).

## 3. Status and lifecycle issues

- If a job or work order is stuck:
  - Verify that recent actions were recorded (status changes, assignments).
  - Look at recent activity or audit history for that job.
- For duplication:
  - Check whether imports or bulk operations were recently performed.

## 4. Data consistency checks

- If multiple users are seeing different information:
  - Confirm that they are in the same organization and context.
  - Check for any known caching or replication delays.

## 5. Communication and follow-up

- Communicate interim workarounds if available (for example, reassigning or recreating a record when safe).
- After resolution:
  - Capture root cause and fix steps.
  - Consider whether additional validations or safeguards are needed in the job lifecycle.
