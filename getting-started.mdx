## Prerequisites

- A Fieldgrid account with access to at least one organization.
- Permissions to create fields and tasks (typically an admin or manager role).

## Step 1 – Log in and select your organization

1. Go to the Fieldgrid web app.
2. Sign in with your organization email.
3. If you have access to multiple organizations, choose the one you want to work in from the organization switcher.

## Step 2 – Create your first field

1. Navigate to the **Fields** area.
2. Click **Create field**.
3. Enter a name, location, and any required metadata (such as crop type or area).
4. Save the field.

At this point, Fieldgrid will treat this as the primary context for tasks, sensor data, and reporting.

## Step 3 – Invite your team

1. Open **Settings → Team**.
2. Invite collaborators by email.
3. Assign them roles based on what they need to do:
   - Field techs focused on completing tasks and logging data.
   - Managers focused on planning work and reviewing reports.
   - Admins with full configuration access.

## Step 4 – Run a simple workflow

To see Fieldgrid in action end-to-end, follow the **Onboard a new farm** guide in the User guides section. It walks through:

- Setting up fields and basic configuration.
- Scheduling and completing a first task.
- Reviewing captured data.
