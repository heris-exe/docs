## Overview

Customers represent the client accounts your organization serves.

Backend module: `fieldgrid-fastapi/app/customers`  
Base path: `/api/v1/customers`

## Example endpoints

- `GET /api/v1/customers` – list customers for the current organization.
- `POST /api/v1/customers` – create a new customer.
- `GET /api/v1/customers/{customerId}` – fetch details for a specific customer.
- `PATCH /api/v1/customers/{customerId}` – update customer information.

Customers sit at the top of the operational hierarchy: they own projects, which in turn own jobs and work orders.
