## Who this is for

This guide is for **technicians and admins** when:

- A technician believes they clocked in/out, but doesn’t see their time.
- Admins can’t find expected time entries in approvals or reports.

## Checks for technicians

1. **Confirm you actually clocked in**
   - Think back to when your shift started:
     - Did you see confirmation that you were “clocked in”?
     - Did you accidentally close the app before confirming?

2. **Check the date and time range**
   - Make sure the list or summary you’re looking at:
     - Is showing the correct dates.
     - Is not filtered to a narrow time window.

3. **Check job context (if your company uses it)**
   - If your company links time to specific jobs:
     - Confirm you were on the right job when you started or stopped the clock.

If you still don’t see your time, note roughly when you started and ended and share that with your admin.

## Checks for admins

1. **Verify date filters**
   - In time or approvals views:
     - Confirm the correct date range.
     - Try expanding the range to include a wider window.

2. **Check technician filters**
   - Make sure you’re:
     - Looking at the right technician.
     - Not limited to a subset of the team.

3. **Look for open (active) sessions**

If a technician forgot to clock out, you may see:

- An active or incomplete entry.
- A very long duration.

Work with the technician to:

- Confirm actual start and end times.
- Correct the entry, according to your internal policies.

4. **Confirm organization and environment**
   - As with jobs, ensure:
     - You and the technician are in the same organization.
     - You’re looking at the correct environment (production vs test).

## When to contact support

Contact Fieldgrid support if:

- A technician is certain they saw a successful clock-in or clock-out message.
- You can’t find any matching entries for them in a reasonable date range.
- You’ve checked filters, teams, and organizations.

Provide:

- Organization name.
- Technician name and email.
- Approximate times they believe they clocked in/out.

For broader context, see **Technician time tracking** and **Admin job lifecycle** guides.
