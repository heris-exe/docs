## Overview

This guide describes the **technician onboarding flow** for users who are joining an existing organization instead of creating a new one.

It covers:

- Accepting invites or requesting to join.
- Providing personal information.
- Waiting for and receiving approval.
- Landing on the technician dashboard.

## Entry points

Technicians typically reach this flow in two ways:

- **Invite-based**
  - They receive an email invite from an admin.
  - Clicking the link takes them into a short “join company” experience.
- **Self-serve join request**
  - They sign up and choose an option like **Join an existing company**.

## Key screens

While the exact layout may evolve, the flow always includes:

- A step to confirm your personal information.
- A step to choose or confirm which company you’re joining.
- A confirmation step to submit your join request.
- A “request sent” screen.
- A success screen once you’ve been approved.

## Step-by-step flow

### 1. Personal information

First, technicians confirm who they are:

- First and last name.
- Contact details.
- Optional information about their role or specialization.

### 2. Select or search for a company

Next, they choose which company to join:

- If they came from an invite link, the company is usually pre‑selected.
- Otherwise, they may:
  - Pick from a list of available companies.
  - Enter an invite code or search by name (depending on how you’ve configured onboarding).

### 3. Confirm join request

Before sending the request, technicians see a confirmation step where they:

- Double‑check the company they’re joining.
- See their expected role (for example, technician).
- Confirm that they want to send a join request.

### 4. Request sent

After submitting, they see a **request sent** screen that clearly explains:

- Their request is pending approval from an admin.
- They’ll gain access once someone on the admin side approves them.

### 5. Approval and success

Once an admin approves the request:

- Technicians can log in and access the technician experience.
- A success screen confirms:
  - Which company they’ve joined.
  - Suggested next steps, like viewing today’s schedule or opening their first job.

### 6. Landing experience

After onboarding is complete, technicians simply sign in and go straight to their technician dashboard for future sessions.
