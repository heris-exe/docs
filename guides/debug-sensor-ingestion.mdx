## Symptoms

You might be here if:

- Sensor charts are flat or missing recent data.
- Alerts are not triggering when expected.
- A customer reports that a device is online but data is not visible in Fieldgrid.

## Quick checks

1. Confirm which **organization**, **field**, and **sensor** are affected.
2. Check whether the issue is isolated to one sensor or affects multiple sensors.
3. Verify time range filters in the UI (e.g., are you looking at the right day).

## Step 1 – Validate sensor status in the UI

1. Navigate to the **Sensors** area.
2. Open the affected sensor.
3. Check:
   - Last seen timestamp.
   - Last reading timestamp and value.

If both are stale, continue to backend and integration checks.

## Step 2 – Check ingestion pipeline

From here, follow the internal **Sensor ingestion pipeline** runbook in the Internal section. At a high level you will:

- Verify that upstream providers are delivering data.
- Inspect ingestion logs for errors.
- Confirm that messages are reaching the processing service and database.

## Step 3 – Communicate with the customer

- Acknowledge the issue and share your initial findings.
- If you have a workaround (manual data entry, alternate sensor), document it.
- Once resolved, explain the root cause in clear, non-technical language.
