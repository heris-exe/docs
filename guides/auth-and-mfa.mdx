## When this guide is useful

Share this guide with anyone who needs to:

- Log in to Fieldgrid for the first time.
- Turn on or complete multi‑factor authentication (MFA).
- Verify their email or reset their password.

Everything here is written from the end‑user’s perspective, without any technical setup steps.

## Signing in

1. **Open the Fieldgrid login page.**
2. Enter the email address you used when you were invited or signed up.
3. Enter your password.
4. Select **Sign in**.

If your email or password is incorrect, you’ll see an error and can try again or use the password reset flow below.

### Creating an account (when self‑service signup is enabled)

If your organization allows self‑service signup:

1. On the login page, switch to the **Create account** option.
2. Fill in your name, company, and email address.
3. Choose a secure password.
4. Submit the form and check your email for any verification or next‑step instructions from Fieldgrid or your admin.

## Multi‑factor authentication (MFA)

MFA adds a second check (such as a code from an app or text message) on top of your password.

### Turning on MFA

If your organization requires or recommends MFA, you’ll see prompts inside Fieldgrid to set it up:

1. After signing in, go to your **profile** or **security** section.
2. Follow the on‑screen steps to connect your preferred MFA method (for example, an authenticator app).
3. Save any recovery codes if they’re provided.

Once MFA is enabled, you’ll be asked for a code each time you sign in from a new device or browser.

### Completing an MFA challenge

When MFA is turned on for your account:

1. Sign in with your email and password as usual.
2. On the MFA screen, open your authenticator app (or other MFA method).
3. Enter the current code.
4. If the code is correct, you’ll be taken into Fieldgrid; otherwise you can retry with a fresh code.

If you lose access to your MFA device, contact your Fieldgrid admin or support to regain access.

## Email verification

Email verification helps keep accounts secure and makes sure important notifications reach you.

You may be asked to verify your email when:

- You first create an account.
- You change your email address.

### Verifying your email

1. Look for a message from Fieldgrid in your inbox.
2. Open the email and click the **Verify email** button or link.
3. After a successful verification, you’ll see a confirmation page and can continue into the app.

If you don’t see the email:

- Check your spam or promotions folders.
- Use the **Resend verification email** option in Fieldgrid if it’s available.

## Resetting your password

If you forget your password or want to reset it:

1. On the login page, choose **Forgot password**.
2. Enter the email address associated with your account.
3. Check your inbox for a reset email and click the link inside.
4. Choose a new password and confirm it.
5. Sign in again using your new password.

If no email arrives within a few minutes, double‑check the address you entered, look in your spam folder, and then contact your admin or support if you still can’t find it.
