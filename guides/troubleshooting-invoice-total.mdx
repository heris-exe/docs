## Who this is for

This guide is for **admins and office staff** responsible for creating or reviewing invoices in Fieldgrid.

## Step 1 – Review line items

1. Open the invoice and carefully review:
   - Each line item.
   - Quantities and unit prices.
   - Any discounts, surcharges, or taxes.
2. Ask yourself:
   - Are there extra items that shouldn’t be there?
   - Are any quantities obviously off (for example, an extra zero)?

If a line item is clearly wrong, correct it and see if the total now matches your expectation.

## Step 2 – Check linked time and materials

If your invoice is built from:

- Time entries:
  - Review how many hours were pulled in and at what rate.
  - Confirm that only approved time appears on the invoice.
- Materials:
  - Confirm which parts or materials were included.
  - Cross-check against actual usage on the job.

If you find extra or missing time or materials, adjust either the underlying data (if appropriate) or the invoice line items directly, depending on your process.

## Step 3 – Confirm tax and discount settings

- Look at:
  - Tax rates or rules applied to the invoice.
  - Any discounts (percentage or fixed) that might be affecting the total.
- Ask:
  - Should tax be applied to all items, or only some?
  - Are there promotional or contract discounts that should or should not apply here?

Adjust as needed according to your company’s policies.

## Step 4 – Compare against your agreement or estimate

If the basics look correct but the total still feels off:

- Compare the invoice to:
  - The original quote or estimate.
  - The customer contract for this kind of work.
- Check:
  - Whether scope has changed (extra visits, added work orders, or new materials).
  - Whether change orders have been applied correctly.

## When to contact support

If, after all of these checks:

- Line items look correct.
- Linked time and materials make sense.
- Taxes and discounts are as expected.

But the total still seems wrong, contact Fieldgrid support with:

- Organization name.
- Invoice identifier.
- A brief summary of what you expected versus what you see.

You can also share this guide and the **Admin invoicing and billing** doc with anyone on your team who helps review invoices.
