## Overview

This guide covers the **admin onboarding flow** for new customers:

- Creating a new organization.
- Providing basic company details.
- Customizing the workspace.
- Choosing a billing path.

## Entry points

After a new admin creates an account, Fieldgrid guides them through a short setup flow before they land on the main dashboard.

You’ll see this flow if:

- You’re the first person from your company to sign up.
- Your organization hasn’t been configured yet.

## Step-by-step flow

### 1. Personalization

First, Fieldgrid asks a few questions about you:

- Your name and role.
- What kind of work your team does.
- Any preferences that help tailor the experience.

### 2. Company details

Next, you provide basic company information:

- Company name.
- Industry or vertical.
- Default time zone.
- Optional details like company size.

This is what Fieldgrid uses to create your organization and scope everything (projects, jobs, people) under it.

### 3. Company customization

You can then customize how Fieldgrid looks and behaves for your team, for example:

- Adding your branding or logo (where supported).
- Setting defaults for jobs, notifications, or regions.

### 4. Billing choice

Fieldgrid then asks how you’d like to get started:

- Start a free trial.
- Pick a subscription plan.
- Talk to sales or request a demo.

You’ll see plan options, seats, and modules so you can choose what fits your team.

### 5. Account created and workspace setup

To finish, you’ll see a confirmation page and a few recommended next steps, such as:

- Invite key team members.
- Add your first customer or project.
- Upload important documents or templates.

Once you’ve completed onboarding, you’ll go straight to your admin dashboard the next time you sign in.
