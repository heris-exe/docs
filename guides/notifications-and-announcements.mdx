## Overview

Fieldgrid uses:

- **Notifications** to alert users about events (assignments, comments, approvals, schedule changes).
- **Announcements** to broadcast messages across the organization (or from super admins to all orgs).
- **Activity and audit logs** to track changes and actions.

This guide explains how these surfaces behave for admins and technicians.

## Entry points

- Notifications:
  - Notification icon and dropdown in the app header.
  - Dedicated notification or activity pages where configured.
- Announcements:
  - Admin:
    - `/admin/announcement`
  - Technician:
    - `/technician/announcement`
  - Super admin:
    - `/superadmin/announcements`
- Audit and activity:
  - `/admin/audit-log`
  - `/superadmin/audit-logs`

## Notification behavior

### 1. Receiving notifications

- The frontend subscribes to:
  - Websocket or server-sent events via `useWebSocketNotifications`.
  - Polling endpoints via `useNotifications`.
- Notifications are:
  - Rendered in header dropdowns.
  - Counted as unread until opened.

### 2. Opening and marking read

- When a user clicks a notification:
  - Actions may:
    - Navigate to a job, project, invoice, or other entity.
    - Mark the notification as read via notifications APIs.
- `useNotificationEntityResolver` maps:
  - Notification payloads to concrete app routes.

## Announcement behavior

### 1. Creating announcements (admin and super admin)

- Admins and super admins can:
  - Open an announcement composer.
  - Draft text, rich content, or links.
  - Schedule start and end times.
  - Choose target audiences (org-wide, role-specific, etc.).
- The composed announcements are saved via:
  - `announcements` APIs.

### 2. Viewing announcements (everyone)

- On `/admin/announcement` or `/technician/announcement`:
  - Users see active and relevant announcements.
  - Historical announcements may also be listed.

## Activity and audit logs

- Admins and super admins can:
  - Review activity logs filtered by user, entity, or date.
  - Inspect details of:
    - Who changed what.
    - When, and from where.
- These views rely on:
  - `useActivities`, `useAuditTrail`.
  - Activity and audit APIs in `fieldgrid-fastapi`.
