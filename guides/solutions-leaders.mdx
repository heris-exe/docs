## Who this is for

This page is for **owners, principals, and senior leaders** who care about:

- Profitability and margins.
- Service quality and customer experience.
- Scaling operations without losing control.

## Problems Fieldgrid helps you solve

- You can’t easily answer basic questions like:
  - “Which jobs are at risk this week?”
  - “Where are we making or losing money?”
  - “Which customers or projects are our best bets?”
- Reporting is slow and manual, often built from **spreadsheets and gut feel**.
- You worry about **consistency**: each tech or manager has their own way of doing things.

## How Fieldgrid changes your view

### Real visibility into work in progress

- Dashboards and lists show:
  - Which jobs and projects are open.
  - How work is distributed across customers and teams.
  - Where things are overdue or stuck.
- You can drill in to:
  - See what’s actually happening on-site.
  - Understand what’s blocking progress.

See: **Admin dashboard and navigation** and **Admin job lifecycle**.

### Better control of time, materials, and margins

- Time tracking, inventory, and expenses are linked to real jobs, not separate systems.
- This means:
  - You can see labor and materials by job, customer, or team.
  - You have a more accurate picture of job profitability.
  - Overspending or inefficiencies are easier to spot early.

See: **Technician time tracking**, **Admin inventory management**, and **Technician inventory and expenses**.

### Invoicing that reflects the work you actually did

- When jobs close, invoicing can:
  - Pull in time entries and parts usage.
  - Reflect discounts, extras, or changes in scope.
- As a result:
  - Customers see clear, justified invoices.
  - You spend less time chasing corrections or disputes.

See: **Admin invoicing and billing**.

### Standardization without slowing people down

- Checklists and resources help:
  - Encode your best practices once.
  - Make sure every technician has the same playbook on site.
- Logs and evidence (notes, photos, signatures) mean:
  - You have traceable history for quality, safety, and compliance.

See: **Fieldgrid concepts** and **Technician daily workflow**.

### Leverage AI to understand and act faster

- With Alex, your AI assistant, leaders can:
  - Ask questions like “What happened on this project last month?” or “Where are we seeing repeat issues?”.
  - Get summaries instead of reading through raw logs.
  - Prepare updates for customers or internal meetings more quickly.

See: **AI assistant (Alex)**.
